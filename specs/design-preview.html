About Preview

An editorial story page, not a generic salon bio.

About should feel image-led, spacious, and credible. The cadence should resemble the reference About page: human, refined, and brand-defining.

Founder Story

Built around confidence, comfort, and consistency.

The About page should introduce the care philosophy, salon standards, and the emotional tone of the brand before asking the user to book. It needs to feel intentional and premium, not like a text dump.

Brazilian-inspired care

Warmth without cliché.

The palette and composition should suggest warmth, softness, and trust while avoiding overly colorful spa styling.

Care standards

Visible expertise.

Hygiene, professionalism, client comfort, and transparent communication should all be surfaced as credibility cues.
